Story Background
You are a young child of an unknown gender or age. You have been invited to visit the famed Mr. Bigglesworth Magical Fortress in the clouds above your home. It is unknown how you got there, but perhaps you can find out. You are also unsure of what you want to do at the fortress. You have heard mystical stories and dark whispers of something going on inside the fortress? Care to find out?

You see something resembling a man approaching you. You can not fully grasp if it is fact human or something else. You decide that it is at least male. The man extends his hand, "Welcome to Mr. Bigglesworth Magical Fortress." "My name is Godfrey and I manage the fortress." "Might I know your name"?
